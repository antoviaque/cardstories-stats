Concurrent players

Shows the number of active players at a given time (each player making at least one call to the webservice during any given minute).

Tip: Select an area to zoom.



Active players per week

Shows the number of active players per week, along with the proportion of new players (who registered during the current week) and recurring players (who registered before the current week).

Type


Funnel

Shows the percentage of new visitors who successfully go through each of these steps:

  1. % of new visitors who register
  2. % of previous step passing the loading screen
  3. % of previous step creating a story
  4. % of previous step moving a game to the vote phase themselves
  5. % of previous step moving a game to the result phase themselves
  6. % of previous step joining or creating another game
  7. % of previous step coming back at least once to the game, on the next day or later
  8. Total (% of new visitors who go through all the steps in the right order)
Cohort


Cohorts

Shows the percentage of players still playing after 1, 2, 3 weeks... There is one cohort per week (if you register on week 1, you're in cohort 1).

Enough players?

Shows the proportion of time where there are 3 or more players in the game. It serves to highlight when players find themselves without enough human opponents. The bots aren't counted as players.

Tip: Select an area to zoom.
